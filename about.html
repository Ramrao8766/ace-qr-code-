About ACE Tree Information

Project Overview

The ACE Tree Information project is a web-based platform developed by final-year Computer Science and Engineering students of Agnihotri College of Engineering (ACE). This platform provides valuable information about trees, their types, benefits, and importance in the ecosystem.

Objectives

  • To spread awareness about different types of trees, their characteristics, and environmental benefits.
  • To provide an engaging and informative platform for users to explore tree species.
  • To promote sustainability and the importance of trees in maintaining ecological balance.

Technologies Used

  • HTML/CSS for building the core structure and styling of the website.
  • JavaScript for interactive elements like the image slider.
  • Responsive Web Design to ensure the site is mobile-friendly.
  • Adobe Photoshop for custom graphics and visuals.
